## What is LinkSprout?

LinkSprout is a free, open-source alternative to services like Linktree, Beacons, and Carrd. It runs entirely in your browser, giving you complete control and privacy. Create your page with a live visual editor, download the files, and host it anywhere you want‚Äîfor free, forever.

## How It Works

1.  **Open the Editor:** Go to [linksprout.github.io](https://linksprout.github.io).
2.  **Customize:** Use the intuitive controls to design your page. Add your links, style your profile, and choose your theme.
3.  **Download:** Click the "Download" button to get your site as a single HTML file or a complete ZIP package.
4.  **Deploy:** Upload the downloaded file(s) to your hosting provider. See our simple guide for **[deploying to GitHub Pages](deploy.md)**.
